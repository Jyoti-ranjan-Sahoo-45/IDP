About Intelligent Document Processing

What is Intelligent Document Processing?

Intelligent Document Processing (IDP) is a technology that uses artificial intelligence and machine learning to automatically extract, classify, and process information from various document formats such as PDFs, images, emails, and more. IDP combines optical character recognition (OCR), natural language processing (NLP), and machine learning to understand document contents and extract meaningful information.

Our System's Capabilities

Our Intelligent Document Processing system offers a wide range of capabilities:

  • Document OCR: Extract text from scanned documents, PDFs, and images
  • Entity Recognition: Identify and extract key entities like names, organizations, dates, etc.
  • Sentiment Analysis: Analyze the sentiment expressed in the document
  • Keyword Extraction: Identify the most important terms in the document
  • Text Analytics: Gain insights from document contents
